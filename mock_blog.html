The Best Dive Watches of 2024

Diving watches have always been a staple in the horological world. Today, we're looking at some top contenders.

First up is the legendary Rolex Submariner 124060. This no-date sub is the purest expression of the dive watch. With its 41mm case, precise chronometer movement, and iconic Oyster bracelet, it's a watch that looks just as good with a tuxedo as it does with a wetsuit. Its history goes back to the 1950s, worn by everyone from Sean Connery's James Bond to professional deep-sea divers. The black dial and cerachrom bezel are unmistakable.

Next we have the Omega Seamaster Diver 300M, another classic. It has a beautiful wave dial, but for me, nothing beats the stark simplicity of the Rolex Submariner.

Finally, the Tudor Black Bay 58 is a great vintage-inspired option that costs less, but lacks the modern polish of its bigger brother Rolex.
